I am a PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the University of Texas at Austin (UT, Austin) since Fall, 2013. I am advised by Dr. Joydeep Ghosh, director of IDEAL at UT, Austin. Prior to joining UT, I worked with Amazon Lab126 for two years, prototyping and developing interesting application and platform software using Machine Learning, Image Processing and Computer Vision for next generation consumer products. I have completed my Masters from University of California, San Diego, majoring in Computer Engineering in 2011. I completed my undergraduate degree (B. Tech) in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur, India. For more details, my CV is available here.

My primary research interests are in Machine Learning focusing on building interpretable models, incorporating noisy/weak supervision and applications to (but not limited to) clinical healthcare.
